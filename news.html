  • 1 Feb 2016

    Fantsuam Foundation to onveil its new web portal.
    by Barnabas Suku

    After months of re-design, developments and consultations, FF is proud to make its portal public from today. This was made possible with the help of the authorities at the Foundation, heads of departments and the staff who each contributed toward suppling information required for the portal.

    With the new Fanstuam Foundation portal, visitors have the opportunity to get all information about the Foundation, obtain recent information about departmental happenings, obtain staff profile, browse services some of the Foundation's departments offer, register for our diploma or certificate courses and check/print your result for our graduated ZittNet staff.

  • 15 Feb 2016

    Jamb closes its registration.
    by Samaila Ibrahim

    Following the massive jamb registration done, prospective students were taken by surprise after they learnt jamb registration was closed.

    With the new development, jamb has asked students to return back for a print out of forms informing them of their centers.
